Remarks

Shape:

The shape is symmetric across the central vertical / horizontal axis of the boundingRect.

Enumerator:

The enumerator does not repeat any points.

Remarks

Note that when setting this, it may not be possible to draw an IsometricHexagon with that bounding rect. The rect will be made narrower (height unchanged) - moving the left and right sides by equal amounts - until an IsometricHexagon can be drawn with that bounding rect.
